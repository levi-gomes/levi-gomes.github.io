What is this??

Well, that's a stupid question, isn't it? This is a blog, what else would it be? Jokes aside, this is a blog mostly about gamedev - although I can't promise I won't write about anything else. I'll be making all sorts of posts, but one of the main focuses of this blog will be "Islands", the game I'm currently working on. On those posts, I'll talk about what I've been doing to the game, and I'll share some things I have learned doing it. By the way, the game isn't open source, but my aim is to write these posts as a deep dive into the mechanics of the game. For those of you who aren't interested in such technical information, you can just as well watch the short devlogs I'll be posting on YouTube alongside the posts in this blog. Also, I can't guarantee consistent posts, there might very well be periods of one to two months in which I don't post anything - or upload to YouTube - as I'm still a student and of course school comes first. So I'll try to work on the game and make posts whenever I can, but no promises. Anyway, I hope you'll enjoy my posts and following along with the development of "Islands". Cheers!
- Leviathan, 14/09/2022
